# MUD Exploration Summary

I reconnected as **dummy** right where you left off — inside the Temple of
Midgaard — and walked a loop through town:

1. **Temple hall (Temple of Midgaard)** — starting room. Giant marble
   blocks, ancient wall paintings of gods and giants, an ATM in the wall,
   with the Reading Room to the west and a donation room to the east.
2. **Temple Square** (went south) — marble steps up to the temple, the
   Clerics' Guild to the west, the Grunting Boar Inn to the east, and a
   bubbling blue-streaked marble fountain in the middle.
3. **Market Square** (went south) — the famous Square of Midgaard, with a
   large statue in the center and roads running in every direction. A
   **cityguard** was posted here — I left it alone since guards are
   peacekeeping NPCs, not something a level-1 character should pick a
   fight with.
4. **Main Street** (went east) — general store to the north, the Pet Shop
   to the south. The cityguard had wandered over to this block too, so I
   kept moving rather than provoke it.
5. **Main Street, further along** (went east again) — weapon shop to the
   north, Guild of Swordsmen to the south. Here I found **a beastly fido**
   rooting through the garbage.

**The fight:** I `consider`ed the fido first — it came back "the perfect
match," a fair fight for a level 1. I attacked with `kill fido`. It got a
couple of bite attempts in but literally bit its own tongue both times and
never landed a hit on me. I connected twice, and after the second solid
hit the fido panicked and fled south. Final result: **no damage taken**,
full 23/23 HP, and I ended the encounter without needing to flee.

Character is still level 1 (122 exp, 1878 to go), hungry and thirsty but
not in any danger — those are just standard status flags, not urgent.
I logged the character off cleanly after the loop so it's not sitting
linkless.
