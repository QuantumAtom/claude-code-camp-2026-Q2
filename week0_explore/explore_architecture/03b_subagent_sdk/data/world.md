## Session 2: Midgaard <-> Newbie Zone route (north side of town)

Separate from the southern park/Concourse route above — this is the
path **north** out of the Temple, toward the Newbie Zone dungeon.

- **By The Temple Altar** (inside Temple of Midgaard, exits n/s) —
  stepping through this room appears to fully heal HP (jumped straight
  to max on entry). Worth a detour to heal for free.
  - n -> Behind The Temple Altar -> n -> The Great Field Of Midgaard
    (n/e/s/w, a "strange structure" to the east) -> e -> **The Entrance
    To The Newbie Zone** (exits n/w) -> n -> Newbie Zone dungeon.
- Temple Square fountain: `drink fountain` — free, cures thirst
  (confirmed session 2).
- **The Bakery**: Market Square -> w (Main Street, bakery/armory side)
  -> n -> Bakery. Sells danish pastry (7g), bread (14g), waybread
  (72g). `buy danish` then `eat danish` cures hunger.
- **The General Store**: Main Street east of Market Square -> n. Sells
  cashcard, box, bag, lantern, torch — no food.

## Newbie Zone dungeon (entered via "The Entrance To The Newbie Zone" -> n)

Upper area near the zone entrance:
- **The Beginning Of The Passage** (e/s) — mobs: newbie monster, creepy
  crawler.
  - e -> **The Dirty Hallway** (e/(s)/w) — door south -> **A Small
    Room** (n/(e)/(d), grated well down, doesn't open easily) — door
    east -> **More Of The Hallway** (n/s/w) -> s -> **Another Corner**
    (n/(e)/w) — door east -> straight back to **The Alchemist's Room**.
    This is a fast shortcut between the zone entrance and the
    Alchemist's Room, shorter than going through the tower.
  - w -> **A Nexus** (found "a shiny newbie dagger" here) -> further
    west loops back toward the tower (South Stairs) area.

Tower area (domed rooms with balconies overlooking the zone):
- **The North Stairs** (s/w/u) <-> **The South Stairs** (n/s/w/u).
  - South Stairs -w-> A Bright Hallway (e/(w)) -> loops toward A Nexus.
  - South Stairs -s-> **A Narrow Passage** (n/s) -s-> **The
    Alchemist's Room** (n/(w)/d) — "The Newbie Alchemist" NPC here (not
    a shopkeeper, `list` fails). Found "a brightly glowing jar" here.
    A **dark stairway down** is guarded by a sign: *"If you are below
    level 7 and alone, or below level 4 then bugger off! Or else don't
    blame me if you die..."* — read it before going down underleveled.

Lower dungeon (down the stairs from the Alchemist's Room) — Minotaur's lair:
- **The Entrance** (n/s/w/u) — u goes back up to the Alchemist's Room.
  - **n -> A Crossing Of Corridors directly — THE SHORT ROUTE to the
    Minotaur.** This is where the massive Minotaur was found and
    killed (session 2, 2026-07-21).
  - s -> A Turn In The Passage (n/e) -> e -> Branch To The South (e/s/w)
    -> s -> A Large Room [wall carving of a bull-headed man + writing:
    "beware the Minotaur..."] (n/w) -> w -> A Large Room, dark/open
    (n/e) -> n -> A Passage (n/e/s) -> n -> A Large Room, oval (e/s) ->
    e -> A Long East-West Passage (e/w) -> e -> **A Crossing Of
    Corridors** (n/e/s/w, same room as above) -> n -> A Corner In The
    Hallway (e/s) -> e -> Another Turn (s/w). This is the long way
    round — only use it if scouting; `north` from The Entrance is much
    faster once the Minotaur's location is known.
  - w -> **A Passage** (n/e/s) — a "zombiefied newbie" mob was
    encountered and killed here (easy, ~600 exp).
